# Welcome to SciSynth

> **Multi-Agent Deep Research** — powered by LangGraph + RAG

**SciSynth** is an AI research assistant that retrieves scientific evidence and synthesizes cited answers.

## Two Modes

| Mode | What it does |
|------|-------------|
| ⚡ **Quick Q&A** | Ask a question → retrieve evidence → get a cited answer |
| 🔬 **Deep Research** | Enter a research topic → a team of AI agents plans, researches, writes, and reviews a full report |

## Quick Start

- **Quick Q&A:** Just type your question
- **Deep Research:** Start your message with `/research` followed by your topic

### Examples

**Quick Q&A:**
> What does the literature say about multi-hop retrieval for document QA?

**Deep Research:**
> /research How do transformer-based models handle long-document question answering and what retrieval strategies improve synthesis quality?

---

*Configure your LLM key in `.env` — currently supports any OpenAI-compatible API (Groq, OpenAI, etc.)*
